Nutrition Recommendation

Limit Processed Foods:


- Cut back on sugary drinks, packaged snacks, fried foods, and processed meats. They're high in calories and low in nutrients.

Fill Up on Good Stuff:


- Focus on whole, unprocessed foods like fruits, veggies, whole grains, and lean protein. These keep you fuller for longer.

Cut Back on Calories:


- Aim to eat less than you burn each day. A doctor or dietitian can help you set a safe and realistic calorie goal.

Watch Your Portions:


- Pay attention to how much you eat. Use measuring tools or your hand as a guide to avoid overeating.
