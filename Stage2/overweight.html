Nutrition

Nutrition Recommendation

Healthy Eating Habits:


- Fruits and vegetables (aim for at least 5 servings per day)
- Lean protein sources (chicken, fish, beans, lentils)
- Low-fat or fat-free dairy (milk, yogurt, cheese)

Focus on Calorie Deficit:


- Aim to consume fewer calories than you burn each day to create a calorie deficit for weight management. A doctor or registered dietitian can help determine a safe and appropriate calorie goal.

Limit Processed Foods:


- Reduce processed foods high in unhealthy fats, added sugars, and sodium. These contribute to excess calories and offer minimal nutritional value.

Healthy Cooking Methods:


- Choose healthy cooking methods like grilling, baking, steaming, or stir-frying to minimize added fat and retain nutrients.
