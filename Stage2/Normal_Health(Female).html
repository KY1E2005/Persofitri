Nutrition

Nutrition Recommendation

Macronutrients:


- Aim for around 2,000 calories per day for a healthy weight

- At least 50% of your daily calories should come from carbohydrates. Choose whole grains, fruits, and vegetables over refined carbohydrates like white bread and sugary drinks.

- Aim for 45 grams of protein per day. This is important for building and repairing tissues, and can be found in lean meats, fish, poultry, beans, nuts, and seeds.

Micronutrients:


- Focus on getting enough vitamins and minerals, especially those women are often deficient in, such as: Iron, Calcium, and Vitamin D.

Food Choices:


- Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables (Aim for at least 5 servings per day).

- Choose whole grains (Opt for whole-wheat bread, brown rice, quinoa, and other whole grains over refined options).

Hydration:


- Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Aim for around eight glasses of water daily, adjusting for your activity level and climate.
