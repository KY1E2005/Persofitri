Nutrition

Nutrition Recommendation

Calorie Reduction:


- Aim for a daily calorie deficit of 500-1000 calories to promote weight loss.
- Consult a doctor or registered dietitian to determine a safe and effective calorie
goal based on your individual needs and health conditions.

Macronutrients:


- Include lean protein sources like fish, poultry, beans, and nuts.
- Restrict saturated and trans fats found in processed foods and fried foods.

Micronutrients:


- Ensure sufficient intake of essential vitamins and minerals, especially those often deficient in individuals with Overweight

Healthy Cooking Methods:


- Choose healthy cooking methods like grilling, baking, steaming, or stir-frying to minimize added fat and retain nutrients.
