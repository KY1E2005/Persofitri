Nutrition

Nutrition Recommendation

Focus on a Balanced Diet:


- Fruits and vegetables (aim for at least 5 servings per day)
- Healthy fats (avocado, nuts, seeds, olive oil)
- Lean protein sources (chicken, fish, beans, lentils)

Minimize Processed Foods:


- Limit processed foods that are high in unhealthy fats, added sugars, and sodium. These contribute to empty calories and offer minimal nutritional value.

Portion Control:


- Be mindful of portion sizes to avoid overeating, even healthy foods.
- Use measuring cups and spoons or focus on visual cues like your palm or fist for portion reference.

Hydration:


- Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Aim for around eight glasses of water daily, adjusting for your activity level and climate.
