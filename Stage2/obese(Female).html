Nutrition

Nutrition Recommendation

Calorie Reduction:


- Aim for a daily calorie deficit of 500-1000 calories to promote weight loss.
This can be achieved by reducing calorie intake and increasing physical activity.

Macronutrients:


- Prioritize whole, unprocessed foods over refined options.
Choose complex carbohydrates like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
- Limit refined carbohydrates like white bread, pastries, and sugary drinks.
- Include adequate protein (around 0.8-1 gram per pound of body weight) to support muscle mass and metabolism.

Micronutrients:


- Ensure adequate intake of essential vitamins and minerals, especially those often deficient in individuals with
obesity like vitamin D, calcium, and iron.

Watch Your Portions:


- Pay attention to how much you eat. Use measuring tools or your hand as a guide to avoid overeating.
