Nutrition Recommendation

Focus on Calorie Surplus:


- Aim to consume more calories than you burn each day to promote healthy weight gain. This may require a shift in mindset and eating habits.

Nutrient-Rich Foods:


- Complex Carbohydrates: Provide sustained energy. Choose whole grains like brown rice, quinoa, oats, and whole-wheat bread/pasta.
- Healthy Fats: Important for satiety and nutrient absorption. Include healthy fats from sources like avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil.
- Fruits and Vegetables: Provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Aim for at least 5 servings per day.

Frequent Meals and Snacks:


- Eating smaller, more frequent meals and snacks throughout the day (5-6 times) can help increase overall calorie intake.

High-Calorie, Nutrient-Dense Options:


- Dried fruits and nuts for a portable and energy-boosting snack.
- Full-fat dairy products like milk and yogurt.
