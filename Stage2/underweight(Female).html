Nutrition Recommendation

Focus on Calorie Surplus and Muscle Building:


- Aim to consume more calories than you burn each day to promote healthy weight gain.
This may require a shift in mindset and eating habits.

Macronutrients:


- Continue prioritizing complex carbohydrates but can increase overall intake slightly.
- Include healthy fats in moderation.
- Fruits and Vegetables: Provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Aim for at least 5 servings per day.

Frequent Meals and Snacks:


- Ensure adequate intake of essential vitamins and minerals, especially those often deficient in underweight
individuals like iron, calcium, and vitamin D.

High-Calorie, Nutrient-Dense Options:


- Dried fruits and nuts for a portable and energy-boosting snack.
- Full-fat dairy products like milk and yogurt.
